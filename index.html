About

The Artist began her practice looking for the universal in the human experience. With Let’s build Utopia her aim is to capture the beauty in diversity. At the root of this idea lies the effort to create a hopeful story and to use the synergy effect of bringing people together to shape a more inclusive world.

A project by Svenja Moeller 2019.
